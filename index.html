Who We Are

A Legacy of Trust & Excellence

Founded over 15 years ago by Mr. Vinod Kumar Badgujar, Seva Sarthi Associates is a professionally managed real estate company offering integrated legal advisory and consultation services. We specialize in commercial office spaces and industrial plots, providing comprehensive solutions for rent, lease, sale, and purchase.

With a strong presence in Delhi NCR and operations across India, we assist businesses, investors, and property owners with transparent transactions, strategic guidance, and legally secure real estate solutions.
